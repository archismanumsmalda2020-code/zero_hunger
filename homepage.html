About Us

“The “Zero Hunger – Sharing is Caring” initiative aims to eliminate hunger in local communities by connecting food donors—individuals, NGOs, and restaurants—with those in need. By rescuing surplus food that would otherwise go to waste, the platform ensures that nutritious meals reach underprivileged families, shelters, and community kitchens. Beyond providing food, it fosters a culture of sharing, encourages volunteer participation, and raises awareness about responsible food distribution, nutrition, and sustainability, working together toward a world with zero hunger.

Our Mission

Our mission is to achieve Zero Hunger by building a culture of sharing and community responsibility. We focus on rescuing surplus food from households, restaurants, and events, and distributing it to those in need with dignity and care. Through nutrition awareness, hyper-local food distribution, and volunteer-driven initiatives, we aim to reduce food waste, support underprivileged communities, and create a sustainable system where every meal counts by fostering collaboration between donors, volunteers, and local organizations./p>

Our Works

Food Rescue

Collecting surplus food from restaurants, events, and households and delivering it within 90 minutes. We ensure every rescued meal reaches families quickly, safely, and with dignity.

Community Kitchens

Local volunteers run pop-up kitchens to serve hot meals during festivals and exams. These kitchens also bring communities together, fostering teamwork and compassion.

School Breakfast

Providing nutritious breakfasts to improve learning outcomes and reduce absenteeism. We aim to support healthy growth, concentration, and academic performance for children.

Zero-Waste Drives

Workshops on safe storage, portioning, and composting to minimize food wastage. We educate households and communities on sustainable practices for a greener future.
